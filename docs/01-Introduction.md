# 1. Introduction & Purpose

## 1.1 Purpose of This SOP

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) defines the standard operating procedures to operationalize the **Asset Finance Solution** for leasing computer hardware, ensuring:

- ✅ **Cost-effective** use of the awarded contract
- ✅ **Compliance** with public-sector rules and regulations
- ✅ **Reliable support** for departmental ICT needs
- ✅ **Transparent** asset management and financial controls
- ✅ **Audit readiness** for internal and external audits

The purpose is to guide **ICT and SCM staff** and stakeholders through a **consistent, controlled process** for requesting, deploying, and managing leased computers and peripheral devices across their entire lifecycle.

---

## 1.2 Background

### Contract Award & Context
The Department has awarded a **5-year Asset Finance Solution contract** to a service provider for leasing ICT assets (computers, laptops, peripherals, etc.). This contract followed:

- **Expiration** of the previous lease contract
- **Evaluation** that leasing provides:
  - ✅ Predictable costs
  - ✅ Regular technology refresh cycles
  - ✅ Inherent maintenance services
  - ✅ Improved asset management

### Service Provider
- **Selected Vendor:** COCRE8 Technology Solutions (or equivalent per award)
- **Contract Duration:** 5 years
- **Coverage:** End-user computing devices (laptops, desktops, peripherals)
- **Services Included:** Supply, delivery, support, maintenance per agreed SLAs

### Why This SOP Matters
Previously, equipment sourcing and management was uncoordinated, leading to:
- Inconsistent deployment timelines
- Budget overspends
- Poor asset tracking
- Compliance gaps

This SOP standardizes all processes, ensuring operational excellence and regulatory compliance.

---

## 1.3 Scope & Applicability

### Scope
This SOP covers the **entire lifecycle** of leased computer equipment:

1. **Pre-Kickoff Phase:** Contract award acceptance and preparations
2. **Inception Phase:** Formal kickoff and operational setup
3. **Operational Phase:** Day-to-day management of all lease activities
4. **Closeout Phase:** End-of-lease and contract conclusion

### Equipment Covered
- Desktop computers
- Laptops
- Workstations
- Peripherals (monitors, keyboards, mice)
- Related ICT equipment as per lease schedule

### Who Must Follow This SOP

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|-----------------|
| **ICT Personnel** (DGITO, IT Support Teams) | Asset management, deployments, support integration |
| **Supply Chain Management (SCM)** | Procurement, contract management, vendor coordination |
| **Finance (CFO's Office)** | Budget allocation, approvals, invoicing, payments |
| **Contract Management Officials** | Contract administration, compliance monitoring |
| **Human Resources (HR)** | Workforce triggers (new hires, exits) |
| **End-Users** | Device care, incident reporting, policy compliance |
| **Service Provider Personnel** | Equipment supply, maintenance, support delivery |
| **Senior Management** | Strategic oversight, approvals for exceptions |

### Applicability Scope
- **Geographic:** All departmental locations and regional offices
- **Organizational:** All staff levels requiring ICT equipment
- **Functional:** All business units supported by the lease contract
- **Temporal:** For the entire 5-year contract duration and beyond

## 1.4 Compliance Framework

### Applicable Legislation & Policy

This SOP ensures adherence to all relevant prescripts:

**Public Sector Governance:**
- ✅ **Public Finance Management Act (PFMA)** - Asset accountability
- ✅ **Treasury Regulations** - Procurement and financial controls
- ✅ **SCM Delegations & Policies** - Procurement authorization and approval levels
- ✅ **Corporate IT Governance** - Technology standards and strategic alignment
- ✅ **Information Security Policy** - Data protection, encryption, device management
- ✅ **Asset Management Standards** - Tracking, tagging, inventory control
- ✅ **Internal Control Framework** - Segregation of duties, approval workflows
- ✅ **Audit Requirements** - Documentation trails for AG audits

### Key Oversight Conditions
The contract award included specific conditions from oversight bodies (e.g., IBAC):

- **Market Benchmarking:** Requirement to perform competitive price checks for device procurement
- **Compliance Monitoring:** Regular performance reviews and SLA enforcement
- **Financial Controls:** Transparent invoicing and payment authorization
- **Risk Management:** Monitoring and mitigation of contract risks

### Audit Readiness
This SOP supports audit compliance by ensuring:
- ✅ All approvals documented
- ✅ Asset registers accurate and reconciled
- ✅ Invoices verified before payment
- ✅ SLA performance tracked
- ✅ Changes formalized and approved
- ✅ Roles and accountability clearly defined
